  ### Adding Staff

  
  <p>
    All staff needs to bind with posts. A staff can be bound with multiple
    companies, departments and posts. During login, you can select which company
    to log in to, and after login, you can switch companies by clicking company
    name at the upper-left corner.
  </p>
  
  
  <p className="li-title">Procedure</p>
  
  
  <ol>
    <li>
      Log in to the supOS platform, and then click{" "}
      <img src="https://freezonex.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/7.png" alt="" />{" "}
      to go to the design center.
    </li>
    <li>
      Select{" "}
      <strong>
        <strong>Organizational Structure</strong>
      </strong>{" "}
      &gt; <strong>Personnel Management</strong>
      &gt; <strong>Post</strong>.
    </li>
    <li>
      Select a post, and then click <strong>New </strong>on the right side.
    </li>
    <li>
      Enter information of the staff, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.
    <p className="info">
    Select <strong>Create User Account</strong> to set a username and password
    for the staff.
  </p></li>
  </ol>
